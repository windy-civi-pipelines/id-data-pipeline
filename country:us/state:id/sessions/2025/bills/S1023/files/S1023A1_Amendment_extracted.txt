SENATE AMENDMENT TO S.B. NO. 1023
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 3
2 On page 2 of the printed bill, delete lines 9 through 11, and insert:
3 "(3) "Medical intervention" means a procedure, treatment, device, drug
4 injection, medication, or action taken to diagnose, prevent, or cure a dis-
5 ease or alter the health or biological function of a person.".
6 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 4
7 On page 3, following line 38, insert:
8 "(9) The prohibition on medical interventions shall not apply to any
9 situation where personal protective equipment, devices, items, or cloth-
10 ing are required by employers in the public or private sectors based on tra-
11 ditional and accepted industry standards, health or personal safety proto-
12 cols, or federal regulations, including occupational safety and health ad-
13 ministration rules. These exemptions to the prohibition on medical inter-
14 ventions shall not apply to or include any vaccines, mask requirements, or
15 other medical interventions introduced during the COVID-19 pandemic."
16 ; and in line 39, delete "(8) (9)" and insert: "(8) (10)".
