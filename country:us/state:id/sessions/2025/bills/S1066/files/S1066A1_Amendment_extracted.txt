LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Moved by Toews
Seconded by Carlson
IN THE SENATE
SENATE AMENDMENT TO S.B. NO. 1066
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 1
2 On page 1 of the printed bill, in line 25, following "disclosure." in-
3 sert: "A breach of the security of the system of an agency, individual, or
4 commercial entity does not include the acquisition of data from any source
5 other than systems maintained by the agency, individual, or commercial en-
6 tity, or a service provider acting on behalf of such agency, individual, or
7 commercial entity."; following line 29, insert:
8 "(4) "Encryption" includes standard encryption, which scrambles data
9 and requires a digital key to unscramble such data, and any other technique
10 that translates or substitutes unusable data for personal information and
11 requires a token, key, or other protective process to convert the translated
12 or substituted data to personal information.";
13 and in line 30, delete "(4)" and insert: "(4) (5)".
14 On page 2, in line 9, delete "(5)" and insert: "(5) (6)"; in line 12,
15 delete "either" and insert: "either"; also in line 12, delete "or" and in-
16 sert: "or and"; delete lines 23 and 24; in line 25, delete "(g)" and insert:
17 "(f)"; in line 26, delete "(h)" and insert: "(g)"; and in line 31, delete
18 "(6)" and insert: "(6) (7)".
19 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 2
20 On page 3, in line 37, delete "thirty-six (36)" and insert: "twelve
21 (12)".
22 AMENDMENT TO THE BILL
23 On page 4, following line 11, insert:
24 "SECTION 4. That Section 28-51-107, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
25 hereby amended to read as follows:
26 28-51-107. VIOLATIONS. In any case in which an agency's, commercial
27 entity's or individual's primary regulator has reason to believe that an
28 agency, individual or commercial entity subject to that primary regula-
29 tor's jurisdiction under section 28-51-104(6) (7), Idaho Code, has violated
30 section 28-51-105, Idaho Code, by failing to give notice in accordance
31 with that section, the primary regulator may bring a civil action to en-
32 force compliance with that section and enjoin that agency, individual or
33 commercial entity from further violations. Any agency, individual or com-
34 mercial entity that intentionally fails to give notice in accordance with
35 section 28-51-105, Idaho Code, shall be subject to a fine of not more than
36 twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000) per breach of the security of the
37 system."
38 ; and renumber subsequent sections accordingly.
