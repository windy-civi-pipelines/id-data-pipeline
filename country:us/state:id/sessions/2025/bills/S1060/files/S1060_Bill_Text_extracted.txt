Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1060
BY RESOURCES AND ENVIRONMENT COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE DISTRIBUTION OF WATER; AMENDING CHAPTER 18, TITLE 42, IDAHO
3 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 42-1807, IDAHO CODE, TO PROHIBIT
4 CURTAILMENT UNTIL CERTAIN REQUIREMENTS ARE MET; AND DECLARING AN EMER-
5 GENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 18, Title 42, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 42-1807, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 42-1807. DISTRIBUTION OF WATER BETWEEN DISTRICTS. (1) The director
11 may not curtail surface water usage in a district to satisfy surface or
12 ground water right claims in another district unless the affected surface
13 water users are able to seek relief within their district, which includes ap-
14 propriating surface, groundwater, springs, and any other water source owned
15 and administered by the state of Idaho.
16 (2) Once both the surface and groundwater districts in an individual
17 tributary district are fully operational and administered by the depart-
18 ment, the priority doctrine of first in time, first in right and the conjunc-
19 tive management rules shall apply as it pertains to all surface water, ground
20 water, springs, and any other waters of the state.
21 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2025.
