LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 1
BY MONKS AND RUBEL
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 PROVIDING FOR A JOINT SESSION OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE
3 OF THE FIRST REGULAR SESSION OF THE SIXTY-EIGHTH IDAHO LEGISLATURE FOR
4 THE PURPOSE OF HEARING A MESSAGE FROM THE GOVERNOR.
5 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, the Governor has informed the House of Representatives and the
7 Senate that he desires to deliver a message to a Joint Session of the House
8 of Representatives and the Senate of the First Regular Session of the Sixty-
9 eighth Idaho Legislature in the Chamber of the House of Representatives at 1
10 p.m. on Monday, January 6, 2025.
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the First Regular Ses-
12 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives
13 and the Senate concurring therein, that the House of Representatives and the
14 Senate meet in Joint Session on Monday, January 6, 2025, at 1 p.m. for the
15 purpose of hearing the message from the Governor.
