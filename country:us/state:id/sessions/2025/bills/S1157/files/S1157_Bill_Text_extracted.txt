Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1157
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO ELECTIONS; AMENDING SECTION 34-708A, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PRO-
3 VISIONS REGARDING CERTAIN INDEPENDENT CANDIDATES; AMENDING SECTION
4 34-711A, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE CERTIFICATION
5 OF INDEPENDENT PRESIDENTIAL ELECTORS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
6 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Section 34-708A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
9 amended to read as follows:
10 34-708A. INDEPENDENT CANDIDATES FOR PRESIDENT AND VICE PRESI-
11 DENT. (1) Persons who desire to be independent candidates for the offices
12 of president and vice president must file declarations of candidacy as in-
13 dependent candidates no later than August 1. Such declarations must state
14 that such persons are offering themselves as independent candidates and must
15 declare that they have no political party affiliation. The declarations Any
16 person who desires to be an independent candidate for the office of president
17 must file a declaration of candidacy as an independent candidate no later
18 than August 1. Such declaration must state that such person is an indepen-
19 dent candidate with no political party affiliation and shall have attached
20 thereto a petition signed by one thousand (1,000) qualified electors.
21 (2) The candidates for president and vice president shall be considered
22 as candidates for one (1) office, and only one (1) such petition need be filed
23 for both offices.
24 (3) (2) Signatures on the petitions petition required in this section
25 shall be verified no later than September 1 in the manner prescribed in sec-
26 tion 34-1807, Idaho Code, on a form similar to that used for recall petitions
27 under chapter 17, title 34, Idaho Code, as prescribed by the secretary of
28 state;, except that the petition circulators are not required to be Idaho
29 residents. The secretary of state shall certify candidate qualifications no
30 later than September 1.
31 SECTION 2. That Section 34-711A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
32 amended to read as follows:
33 34-711A. CERTIFICATION OF INDEPENDENT PRESIDENTIAL ELECTORS. In-
34 dependent presidential candidates who have qualified for ballot status
35 pursuant to section 34-708A, Idaho Code, shall certify the names of the vice
36 presidential candidate and of presidential electors to the secretary of
37 state on or before September 1, unless an extension of no more than five (5)
38 days is granted by the secretary of state, in order for them to appear on the
39 general election ballot. The secretary of state shall certify the indepen-
40 dent presidential electors, and the independent candidates for president
2
1 and vice-president, vice president to the county clerks on or before Septem-
2 ber 7.
3 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
4 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
5 July 1, 2025.
