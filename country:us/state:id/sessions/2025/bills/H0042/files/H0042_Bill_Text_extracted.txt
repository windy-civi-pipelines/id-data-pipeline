Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 42
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING SECTION 33-5905, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PRO-
3 VISIONS REGARDING THE IDAHO SCHOOL SAFETY AND SECURITY ADVISORY BOARD;
4 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 33-5905, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 33-5905. IDAHO SCHOOL SAFETY AND SECURITY ADVISORY BOARD. (1) There is
9 hereby established in the office of the state board of education the Idaho
10 school safety and security advisory board. The advisory board shall consist
11 of thirteen (13) six (6) members as follows:
12 (a) Four (4) Two (2) members appointed by the governor as follows:
13 (i) One (1) parent of a student who attends an Idaho public
14 school; person currently employed as an emergency responder; and
15 (ii) One (1) teacher who teaches in an Idaho public school member
16 at large;
17 (iii) One (1) representative of a local school board; and
18 (iv) One (1) representative of school superintendents;
19 (b) The state superintendent of public instruction or the superinten-
20 dent's designee;
21 (c) One (1) representative from appointed by the state board of educa-
22 tion; and
23 (d) One (1) representative from the Idaho state police;
24 (e) One (1) representative from the Idaho chiefs of police association;
25 (f) One (1) representative from the Idaho sheriffs' association;
26 (g) One (1) representative from the Idaho office of emergency manage-
27 ment;
28 (h) One (1) representative from the Idaho fire chiefs association; and
29 (i) (d) Two (2) representatives from the state legislature that shall
30 include one (1) member from the senate appointed by the president pro
31 tempore of the senate and one (1) member from the house of representa-
32 tives appointed by the speaker of the house of representatives.
33 (2) The members of the advisory board shall serve the following terms:
34 (a) The gubernatorial appointees shall serve terms of three (3) years.
35 (b) (2) All other members shall serve terms of two (2) years and, if ap-
36 pointed, shall remain in service after the end of a term at the pleasure of
37 the respective appointing body provided in subsection (1) of this section
38 until such member is reappointed or a new member is appointed.
39 (3) A vacancy on the advisory board shall be filled in the same manner as
40 the original appointment and for the balance of the unexpired term.
41 (4) The advisory board shall appoint a chairperson from among its mem-
42 bers for a term certain.
2
1 (5) The members of the advisory board shall be compensated as provided
2 in section 59-509(b), Idaho Code.
3 (6) The advisory board shall meet at least annually, but may meet more
4 frequently subject to the call of the chairperson.
5 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
6 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
7 July 1, 2025.
