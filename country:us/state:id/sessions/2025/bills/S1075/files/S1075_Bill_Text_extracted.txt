Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1075
BY LOCAL GOVERNMENT AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO MUNICIPALITIES; AMENDING SECTION 50-707A, IDAHO CODE, TO RE-
3 VISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE ELECTION OF COUNCILMEN BY DISTRICTS; AND
4 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 50-707A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 50-707A. ELECTION OF COUNCILMEN BY DISTRICTS. (1) Any city having
9 fewer than one hundred thousand (100,000) twenty-five thousand (25,000)
10 inhabitants based upon on the most recent federal decennial census may, by
11 ordinance, provide for districts and the election of councilmen by dis-
12 tricts. Upon the adoption of such an ordinance and at least one hundred
13 twenty (120) days prior to each general election, the governing body of the
14 city shall establish the territory of council districts in accordance with
15 this section. Any city having more than one hundred thousand (100,000)
16 twenty-five thousand (25,000) inhabitants based upon on the most recent fed-
17 eral decennial census shall establish districts and shall elect councilmen
18 by districts for districts so established. Districts shall be established
19 no later than one hundred twenty (120) days prior to the general election
20 following the date that election precincts are established pursuant to the
21 provisions of section 34-301, Idaho Code.
22 (2) Each district shall consist of one (1) or more contiguous election
23 precincts as established pursuant to the provisions of chapter 3, title 34,
24 Idaho Code, and each district shall, to the nearest extent possible, con-
25 tain the same number of people based upon on the most recent federal decen-
26 nial census.
27 (3) Each city establishing districts for the election of councilmen by
28 districts shall establish the number of districts corresponding to the num-
29 ber of council seats determined by the city pursuant to section 50-701, Idaho
30 Code, or for any city having a governing body governed by the provisions of
31 sections 50-801 through 50-812, Idaho Code, the number of council seats de-
32 termined by the city pursuant to section 50-805, Idaho Code.
33 (4) Upon establishment of city election districts, council members are
34 to be elected by the electors of the said geographic district, and any can-
35 didate must be a resident of said geographic district. For cities with fewer
36 than one hundred thousand (100,000) twenty-five thousand (25,000) inhabi-
37 tants that establish districts by ordinance, the council shall determine,
38 not less than ninety (90) days before the next general election, the method
39 of the implementation of this ordinance.
2
1 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
2 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
3 July 1, 2025.
