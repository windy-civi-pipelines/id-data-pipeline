Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 17
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING THAT ALL
3 TEMPORARY AND PENDING RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE POLICE, THE DIVISION OF
4 OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL LICENSES, THE IDAHO PUBLIC UTILITIES
5 COMMISSION, THE IDAHO SECRETARY OF STATE, THE DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRA-
6 TION, AND THE IDAHO STATE LOTTERY HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE
7 HOUSE STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE, WITH AN EXCEPTION.
8 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
9 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
10 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
11 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
12 letin; and
13 WHEREAS, the House State Affairs Committee reviewed temporary and pend-
14 ing rules adopted by the Idaho State Police, the Division of Occupational
15 and Professional Licenses, the Idaho Public Utilities Commission, the Idaho
16 Secretary of State, the Department of Administration, and the Idaho State
17 Lottery.
18 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
19 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
20 Legislature, that all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State
21 Police, the Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses, the Idaho
22 Public Utilities Commission, the Idaho Secretary of State, the Department
23 of Administration, and the Idaho State Lottery, pursuant to the Administra-
24 tive Procedure Act and submitted through the Office of the Administrative
25 Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for review during the 2025 legislative
26 session have been reviewed and approved by the House State Affairs Commit-
27 tee with the exception of IDAPA 31.41.01, Idaho Public Utilities Commission,
28 Customer Relations Rules for Telephone Corporations Providing Services in
29 Idaho Subject to Customer Service Regulation by the Idaho Public Utilities
30 Commission (The Telephone Customer Relations Rules), Docket No. 31-4101-
31 2401, Section 205., Subsection 02., only, which was not approved.
