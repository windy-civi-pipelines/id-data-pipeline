Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1091
BY EDUCATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; AMENDING SECTION 33-5605, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A
3 PROVISION REGARDING THE DISTRIBUTION OF CERTAIN APPROPRIATED MONEYS TO
4 SCHOOL DISTRICTS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
5 DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Section 33-5605, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
8 amended to read as follows:
9 33-5605. EDUCATION OPPORTUNITY RESOURCE ACT -- RULEMAKING. (1) The
10 state department board of education shall distribute appropriated moneys
11 to districts for reimbursement for the cost of internet service and other
12 allowable services in accordance with the methodology established by the
13 education opportunity resource committee.
14 (2) The commission for libraries shall distribute appropriated moneys
15 to Idaho public libraries for reimbursement for the cost of internet service
16 and other allowable services in accordance with the methodology established
17 by the education opportunity resource committee and shall provide techni-
18 cal, E-rate, and security contracting, procurement guidance, and assistance
19 to Idaho public libraries at any such entity's request.
20 (3) The state board of education may:
21 (a) Promulgate rules in compliance with chapter 52, title 67, Idaho
22 Code, to implement the provisions of this chapter. In promulgating
23 such rules, the board shall collaborate with the education opportunity
24 resource committee;
25 (b) Authorize funding increases for internet service levels when a dis-
26 trict consistently exceeds utilization benchmarks, as established by
27 the education opportunity resource committee, during school or busi-
28 ness days and hours, provided adequate funding is available; and
29 (c) Provide technical, E-rate, and security contracting, procurement
30 guidance, and assistance to districts at any such entity's request.
31 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
32 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
33 July 1, 2025.
