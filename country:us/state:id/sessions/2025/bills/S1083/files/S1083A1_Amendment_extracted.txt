Moved by Anthon

IN THE SENATE
SENATE AMENDMENT TO S.B. NO. 1083
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 1
2 On page 2 of the printed bill, in line 27, delete "and a water meter
3 capable of measuring"; in line 28, delete "the total volume diverted is
4 installed at the point or points of diversion"; and in line 30, following
5 "subdivision." insert: "For the purposes of this section, the term "in-home
6 use" means the utilization of water within a residence or household, includ-
7 ing all activities that require water, such as drinking, cooking, bathing,
8 and cleaning within and around the household. It does not include irrigation
9 of lawns, gardens, landscaping, pastures, or other open spaces.".
10 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 2
11 On page 3, in line 6, following "uses" insert: ", as defined in section
12 42-111, Idaho Code,".
13 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 4
14 On page 4, in line 22, delete "collects" and insert: "agrees to collect".
