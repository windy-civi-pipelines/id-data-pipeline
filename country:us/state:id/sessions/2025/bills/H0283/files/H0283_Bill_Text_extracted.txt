Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 283
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO STATE GOVERNMENT AND STATE AFFAIRS; AMENDING CHAPTER 24, TITLE
3 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 67-2407, IDAHO CODE, TO
4 PROVIDE THAT THE GOVERNOR SHALL NOT BILL OTHER DEPARTMENTS OR OFFICERS
5 FOR PERSONNEL EMPLOYED BY THE GOVERNOR; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND
6 PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 24, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 67-2407, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 67-2407. GOVERNOR -- PERSONNEL. Notwithstanding any other provision
12 of law to the contrary, the governor shall not bill other state departments
13 or constitutional officers for personnel employed by the governor.
14 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
16 July 1, 2025.
