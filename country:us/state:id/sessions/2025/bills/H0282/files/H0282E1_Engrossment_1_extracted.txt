Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 282, As Amended in the Senate
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PROPERTY; AMENDING SECTION 55-1002, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVI-
3 SIONS REGARDING HOMESTEAD PROPERTY; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PRO-
4 VIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 55-1002, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 55-1002. FROM WHAT PROPERTY SELECTED. If the owner is married, the
9 homestead may consist of the community or jointly owned property of the
10 spouses or the separate property of either spouse: Provided, that the same
11 premises may not be claimed separately by the husband and wife with the ef-
12 fect of increasing the net value of the homestead available to the marital
13 community beyond the amount specified in section 55-1003, Idaho Code. Each
14 spouse may separately claim a homestead exemption in the amount specified in
15 section 55-1003, Idaho Code. When the owner is not married, the homestead
16 may consist of any of his or her property.
17 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
18 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
19 July 1, 2025.
