Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 357
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PUBLIC FUNDS; AMENDING CHAPTER 8, TITLE 57, IDAHO CODE, BY THE
3 ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 57-829, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS
4 REGARDING THE AMERICA250 COMMEMORATION FUND AND THE AMERICA250 ADVI-
5 SORY COMMITTEE; PROVIDING A SUNSET DATE; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 8, Title 57, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 57-829, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 57-829. AMERICA250 COMMEMORATION FUND -- AMERICA250 ADVISORY COMMIT-
11 TEE. (1) There is hereby created in the state treasury the America250 commem-
12 oration fund for the purpose of funding Idaho's participation in celebrating
13 the two hundred fiftieth anniversary of America's founding. All moneys in
14 the fund are to be used only for the purposes provided in this section, and
15 shall be expended only as directed and approved by the America250 advisory
16 committee pursuant to the provisions of subsection (4) of this section.
17 (2) Moneys in the fund shall include:
18 (a) Any amounts appropriated by the legislature for the purposes de-
19 scribed by this section; and
20 (b) Gifts, grants, and other donations.
21 (3) The state treasurer shall invest the idle moneys of the America250
22 commemoration fund, and the interest earned on such investments shall be
23 retained by such fund. All unobligated funds remaining in the America250
24 commemoration fund on June 30, 2027, shall be transferred by the state con-
25 troller on such date into the general fund.
26 (4)(a) There is hereby created the America250 advisory committee that
27 shall be comprised of seven (7) members, and shall include the director
28 of the state historical society, the state treasurer, one (1) member
29 appointed by the governor, and four (4) members appointed by the leg-
30 islative council. The legislative council shall select the chair of the
31 committee. The America250 advisory committee shall direct and approve
32 all distributions of funds from the America250 commemoration fund.
33 The America250 advisory committee shall meet as least annually at such
34 place and time as it determines and may meet as often as necessary to
35 discharge the duties imposed on it.
36 (b) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the divi-
37 sion of financial management shall approve the spending authority of
38 the America250 commemoration fund for any spending directive that is
39 approved by the America250 advisory committee for any state entity in
40 the same manner as the approval process for funds that are not congniz-
41 able pursuant to section 67-3516(2), Idaho Code. Spending authority
42 approved pursuant to this subsection shall only be made for each state
2
1 entity that does not have sufficient authority within such entity's
2 current appropriation from its miscellaneous revenue fund or other al-
3 lowable fund to comply with the committee's spending directive. If a
4 state entity does not have an allowable miscellaneous fund from which to
5 expend moneys, the state entity shall establish a miscellaneous revenue
6 fund to facilitate the spending directive of the committee.
7 (c) The state treasurer, on behalf of the council, shall record and
8 track all revenues and expenditures by fund sources pursuant to subsec-
9 tion (2) of this section for each approved purpose and to ensure that
10 moneys are not allocated in excess of available revenue.
11 SECTION 2. The provisions of Section 1 of this act shall be null, void
12 and of no force and effect on and after July 1, 2027.
13 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
15 passage and approval.
