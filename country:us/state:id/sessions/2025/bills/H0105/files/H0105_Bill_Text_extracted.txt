Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 105
BY AGRICULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO VETERINARY PRACTICES; AMENDING SECTION 54-2113, IDAHO CODE,
3 TO REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING CORPORATE VETERINARY PRACTICES; AND
4 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 54-2113, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 54-2113. CORPORATE PRACTICE. (1) A veterinary medical practice may be
9 conducted only as a sole proprietorship, as a partnership or as a profes-
10 sional entity as defined in part 9, chapter 21, title 30, Idaho Code, or as
11 any other entity as defined by part 1, chapter 21, title 30, Idaho Code. No
12 business corporation, other than a professional entity, shall be organized
13 for the practice of veterinary medicine or shall provide veterinary medical
14 services.
15 (2) A not-for-profit corporation may own property in connection with
16 a veterinary medical facility or animal shelter, provided that an actively
17 licensed veterinarian makes all the decisions pertaining to diagnosis, care
18 and treatment of the patients.
19 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
21 July 1, 2025.
