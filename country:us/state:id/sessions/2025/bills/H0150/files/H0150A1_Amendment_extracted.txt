SENATE AMENDMENT TO H.B. NO. 150
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 2

3 "out the consent of the other spouse. Either spouse may execute a power of
4 attorney authorizing the other spouse to convey or encumber the homestead on
5 behalf of the principal. Consent shall be evidenced by:
6 (1) Each spouse executing and acknowledging the instrument by which the

8 (2) A deed from one spouse to the other spouse establishing the home-
9 stead property as the sole and separate property of the grantee spouse.".
