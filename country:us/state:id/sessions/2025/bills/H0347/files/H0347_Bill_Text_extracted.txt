5 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,

11 June 30, 2026:

14 COSTS EXPENDITURES OUTLAY TOTAL

20 FROM:

22 Fund $6,700 $6,700
23 GRAND TOTAL $36,500 $402,800 $29,000 $468,300
24 SECTION 2. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY. There is hereby reappropriated
25 to the Office of the Secretary of State any unexpended and unencumbered bal-
26 ance appropriated to the Office of the Secretary of State for election in-
27 tegrity audits and elections system upgrades for fiscal year 2025 for the pe-
28 riod July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026. The Office of the State Controller
29 shall confirm the reappropriation amounts, by fund, expense class, and pro-
30 gram, with the Legislative Services Office prior to processing the reappro-
31 priation authorized herein.
32 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
33 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
34 July 1, 2025.
