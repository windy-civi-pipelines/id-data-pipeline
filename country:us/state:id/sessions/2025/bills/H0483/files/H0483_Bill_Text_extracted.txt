Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 483
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION AND FUNDING FOR THE STATE LIQUOR DIVISION; AP-
3 PROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE STATE LIQUOR DIVISION FOR FISCAL
4 YEAR 2026; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
7 there is hereby appropriated to the State Liquor Division the following
8 amounts to be expended according to the designated expense classes from the
9 Liquor Control Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026:
10 FOR:
11 Personnel Costs $57,400
12 Operating Expenditures $147,000
13 Capital Outlay $439,900
14 TOTAL $644,300
15 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
17 July 1, 2025.
