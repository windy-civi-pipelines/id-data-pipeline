LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 105
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND DECLARING SUPPORT FOR THE RECEIV-
3 ING OF FEDERAL FUNDS FOR PURPOSES OF SUPPORTING ADOPTION IN THE STATE OF
4 IDAHO.
5 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, it is the intent of the Legislature to provide an avenue for
7 the State of Idaho to receive federal funds for the purpose of adoption edu-
8 cation, adoption awareness, and the improvement of birth mother support and
9 care; and
10 WHEREAS, receiving such federal funds would allow the State of Idaho to
11 receive back a portion of Idaho taxpayer money to be used in a manner that
12 aligns with Idaho's pro-life values; and
13 WHEREAS, the Legislature recognizes the value of providing a healthy
14 and welcoming environment and advancing the health and well-being of birth
15 mothers who choose adoption and making Idaho the most adoption-friendly
16 state in the nation while investing public moneys in the creation of future
17 Idaho families; and
18 WHEREAS, the Idaho Department of Health and Welfare engaging in actions
19 supporting an informed choice by Idaho birth mothers to participate in adop-
20 tion would further these aims.
21 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the First Regular Ses-
22 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the Senate and the House of Rep-
23 resentatives concurring therein, that the Legislature declares its support
24 for the receiving of federal funds for adoption education, adoption aware-
25 ness, and the improvement and expansion of birth mother support and care.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of the Legislature support the
27 use of such funds, if received, by the Idaho Department of Health and Wel-
28 fare for the administration, oversight, management, and reporting of pro-
29 grams related to adoption education resources, emotional support resources,
30 physical and medical support, and adoption-related legal support.
