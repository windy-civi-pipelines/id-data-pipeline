Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1164, As Amended
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO BUILDING CODE ACT; AMENDING CHAPTER 41, TITLE 39, IDAHO
3 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 39-4117, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE
4 FOR THE PROCESSING OF BUILDING PERMITS AND TIMELY REVIEW; AND DECLARING
5 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 41, Title 39, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 39-4117, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 39-4117. PROCESSING BUILDING PERMITS -- TIMELY REVIEW. (1) A local
11 government that requires building permits shall make available a document
12 that describes in detail the requirements of its building permit process.
13 The local government shall provide the document to the public on its website
14 and in physical form upon request.
15 (2) An applicant for a building permit shall make a good faith effort to
16 submit a complete application containing the necessary documentation re-
17 quired by a local government as specified in the document required pursuant
18 to subsection (1) of this section. If an application is deemed incomplete,
19 the local government shall, within ten (10) business days of receipt of a
20 residential building permit application and within twenty (20) business
21 days of receipt of a commercial building permit application, provide writ-
22 ten notice to the applicant specifying any missing information necessary to
23 proceed.
24 (3) Upon receipt of a submission or a revised submission following an
25 incomplete determination, a local government shall have ten (10) business
26 days for a residential building permit and twenty (20) business days for a
27 commercial building permit to review the application or the revised submis-
28 sion for completeness. No later than the conclusion of the applicable ten
29 (10) or twenty (20) business day review period, the local government shall
30 provide written notice of determination on the completeness of the appli-
31 cation. A local government's determination that an application is complete
32 shall not constitute approval but shall authorize the application to proceed
33 to formal plan review.
34 (4) An applicant and a local government may agree in writing to an ex-
35 tension of the timelines specified in this section if additional time is
36 needed for the completeness determination. Prior to any such agreement, a
37 local government shall provide written notice to an applicant explaining
38 that an extension is needed.
39 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
40 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
41 July 1, 2025.
