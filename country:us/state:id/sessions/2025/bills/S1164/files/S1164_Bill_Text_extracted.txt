Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1164
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IDAHO BUILDING CODE ACT; AMENDING CHAPTER 41, TITLE 39, IDAHO
3 CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION 39-4117, IDAHO CODE, TO PROVIDE
4 FOR THE PROCESSING OF RESIDENTIAL BUILDING PERMITS AND TIMELY REVIEW;
5 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. That Chapter 41, Title 39, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
8 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
9 ignated as Section 39-4117, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
10 39-4117. PROCESSING RESIDENTIAL BUILDING PERMITS -- TIMELY RE-
11 VIEW. (1) A local government that requires building permits shall make
12 available a document that describes in detail the requirements of its build-
13 ing permit process. The local government shall provide the document to the
14 public on its website and in physical form upon request.
15 (2) An applicant for a building permit shall make a good faith effort
16 to submit a complete application containing the necessary documentation re-
17 quired by a local government as specified in the document required pursuant
18 to subsection (1) of this section. If an application is deemed incomplete,
19 the local government shall, within ten (10) business days of receipt, pro-
20 vide written notice to the applicant specifying any missing information nec-
21 essary to proceed.
22 (3) Upon receipt of a submission or a revised submission following an
23 incomplete determination, a local government shall have ten (10) business
24 days to review the application or the revised submission for completeness.
25 No later than the conclusion of the ten (10) business day review period, the
26 local government shall provide written notice of determination on the com-
27 pleteness of the application. A local government's determination that an
28 application is complete shall not constitute approval but shall authorize
29 the application to proceed to formal plan review.
30 (4) An applicant and a local government may agree in writing to an ex-
31 tension of the timelines specified in this section if additional time is
32 needed for the completeness determination. Prior to any such agreement, a
33 local government shall provide written notice to an applicant explaining
34 that an extension is needed.
35 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
36 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
37 July 1, 2025.
