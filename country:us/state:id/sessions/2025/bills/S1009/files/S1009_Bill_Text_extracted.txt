Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1009
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO MILITIA AND MILITARY AFFAIRS; REPEALING SECTION 46-802, IDAHO
3 CODE, RELATING TO THE PROHIBITION ON UNORGANIZED ASSOCIATIONS AND PA-
4 RADES; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 46-802, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 repealed.
8 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
10 July 1, 2025.
