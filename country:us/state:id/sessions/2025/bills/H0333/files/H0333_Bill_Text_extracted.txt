LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 333
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION AND THE BOARD
3 OF REGENTS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO FOR HEALTH EDUCATION PROGRAMS;
4 APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION AND
5 THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO FOR HEALTH EDUCATION
6 PROGRAMS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AUTHORIZING ADDITIONAL FULL-TIME EQUIV-
7 ALENT POSITIONS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; PRIORITIZING AND REPORTING ON
8 PROGRESS IN WOMEN'S HEALTH; PROVIDING FOR CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND
9 RESTRICTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
10 DATE.
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
12 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
13 there is hereby appropriated to the State Board of Education and the Board
14 of Regents of the University of Idaho for Health Education Programs, the
15 following amounts to be expended according to the designated programs and
16 expense classes from the listed funds for the period July 1, 2025, through
17 June 30, 2026:
18 FOR
19 FOR TRUSTEE AND
20 PERSONNEL BENEFIT
21 COSTS PAYMENTS TOTAL
22 I. BOISE INTERNAL MEDICINE:
23 FROM:
24 General
25 Fund $60,000 $60,000
26 II. EASTERN IDAHO MED RESIDENCIES:
27 FROM:
28 General
29 Fund $240,000 $240,000
30 III. FAMILY MEDICINE RESIDENCIES:
31 FROM:
32 General
33 Fund $120,500 $420,000 $540,500
2
1 FOR
2 FOR TRUSTEE AND
3 PERSONNEL BENEFIT
4 COSTS PAYMENTS TOTAL
5 IV. IDAHO DENTAL EDUCATION PROGRAM:
6 FROM:
7 General
8 Fund $54,000 $54,000
9 V. UNIVERSITY OF UTAH MEDICAL EDUCATION:
10 FROM:
11 General
12 Fund $60,000 $60,000
13 GRAND TOTAL $120,500 $834,000 $954,500
14 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
15 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
16 to the State Board of Education and the Board of Regents of the University of
17 Idaho for Health Education Programs is hereby increased by two (2.00) for the
18 period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
19 SECTION 3. PRIORITIZING AND REPORTING ON PROGRESS IN WOMEN'S HEALTH.
20 The Idaho Graduate Medical Education Committee shall immediately prioritize
21 obstetrics and gynecology (OB/GYN) fellowships in its overall preparation
22 and training of family medicine residents. The Idaho Graduate Medical Edu-
23 cation Committee shall provide a written report to the Joint Finance-Appro-
24 priations Committee detailing progress towards this goal by October 1, 2025.
25 The format and content of this report will be determined in consultation with
26 the Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the Legislative Services Office.
27 SECTION 4. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
28 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
29 strictions specified in this act.
30 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
31 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
32 July 1, 2025.
