LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Moved by Mickelsen
Seconded by Petzke(21)
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE AMENDMENT TO H.B. NO. 78
1 AMENDMENT TO THE BILL
2 On page 12 of the printed bill, following line 38, insert:
3 "SECTION 8. That Section 34-1113, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
4 amended to read as follows:
5 34-1113. IDENTIFICATION AT THE POLLS. All voters shall be required
6 to provide personal identification before voting at the polls or at absent
7 electors polling places as required by section 34-1006, Idaho Code. The per-
8 sonal identification that may be presented shall be one (1) of the following:
9 (1) An Idaho driver's license or identification card issued by the
10 Idaho transportation department, except that such driver's license shall
11 not include an electronic driver's license or identification card issued
12 by the Idaho transportation department pursuant to section 49-315A or
13 49-2444A, Idaho Code;
14 (2) A passport or an identification card, including a photograph, is-
15 sued by an agency of the United States government;
16 (3) A tribal identification card, including a photograph; or
17 (4) A license to carry concealed weapons issued under section 18-3302,
18 Idaho Code, or an enhanced license to carry concealed weapons issued under
19 section 18-3302K, Idaho Code.";
20 and renumber subsequent section accordingly.
21 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 8
22 On page 12, in line 41, delete "5, and 6" and insert: "5, 6, and 8".
23 CORRECTION TO TITLE
24 On page 1, in line 12, delete "AND" and insert: "AMENDING SECTION
25 34-1113, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING IDENTIFICATION AT THE
26 POLLS; AND".
