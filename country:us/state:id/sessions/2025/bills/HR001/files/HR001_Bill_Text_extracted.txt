Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 1
BY REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEM-
3 PORARY AND PENDING RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE TAX COMMISSION HAVE BEEN RE-
4 VIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE HOUSE REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE.
5 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
7 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
8 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
9 letin; and
10 WHEREAS, the House Revenue and Taxation Committee reviewed temporary
11 and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Tax Commission.
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
13 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
14 Legislature, that all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State
15 Tax Commission, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted
16 through the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legisla-
17 ture for review during the 2025 legislative session have been reviewed and
18 approved by the House Revenue and Taxation Committee.
