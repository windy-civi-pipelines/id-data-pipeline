Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 106
BY JUDICIARY AND RULES COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO DEPARTMENT OF LABOR, THE INDUSTRIAL COMMISSION, THE
4 IDAHO DEPARTMENT OF INSURANCE, AND THE DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND
5 PROFESSIONAL LICENSES HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE SENATE
6 COMMERCE AND HUMAN RESOURCES COMMITTEE, WITH EXCEPTIONS.
7 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
9 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
10 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
11 letin; and
12 WHEREAS, the Senate Commerce and Human Resources Committee reviewed
13 temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho Department of Labor, the
14 Industrial Commission, the Idaho Department of Insurance, and the Division
15 of Occupational and Professional Licenses.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
17 in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that all
18 temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho Department of Labor, the In-
19 dustrial Commission, the Idaho Department of Insurance, and the Division of
20 Occupational and Professional Licenses, pursuant to the Administrative Pro-
21 cedure Act and submitted through the Office of the Administrative Rules Co-
22 ordinator to the Legislature for review during the 2025 legislative session
23 have been reviewed and approved by the Senate Commerce and Human Resources
24 Committee with the following exceptions that were not approved:
25 (1) IDAPA 09.01.35, Idaho Department of Labor, Unemployment Insurance
26 Tax Administration Rules, Docket No. 09-0135-2401, Section 011., Sub-
27 section 07., only; and
28 (2) IDAPA 24.37.01, Division of Occupational and Professional
29 Licenses, Rules of the Idaho Real Estate Commission, Docket No.
30 24-3701-2401, Section 150., Subsection 02.a., only.
31 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate Commerce and Human Resources
32 Committee held that the following pending rule docket become effective upon
33 sine die of the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature:
34 IDAPA 24.39.10, Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses, Rules
35 of the Idaho Electrical Board, Docket No. 24-3910-2402.
