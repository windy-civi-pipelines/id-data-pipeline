LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1214
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION; APPROPRI-
3 ATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION FOR FISCAL
4 YEAR 2026; PROVIDING AUTHORIZATION FOR ADDITIONAL FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT
5 POSITIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Public Utilities Commission the follow-
9 ing amounts to be expended according to the designated expense classes from
10 the Public Utilities Commission Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through
11 June 30, 2026:
12 FOR:
13 Personnel Costs $178,700
14 Operating Expenditures 4,100
15 Capital Outlay 4,200
16 TOTAL $187,000
17 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
18 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
19 to the Public Utilities Commission is hereby increased by two (2.00) for the
20 period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
21 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2025.
