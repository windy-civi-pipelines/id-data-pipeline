LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1172
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE; APPROPRIATING
3 ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026;
4 REDUCING MONEYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AND
5 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Department of Commerce for the Broadband
9 Office Program $140,900 to be expended for personnel costs from the General
10 Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
11 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary,
12 there is hereby reduced from the Department of Commerce for the Commerce Pro-
13 gram $140,900 for personnel costs from the General Fund for the period July
14 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
15 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
17 July 1, 2025.
