Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 474
BY HAWKINS
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE IMMIGRATION STATUS AND NATIONALITY OF ARRESTED INDIVIDUALS;
3 AMENDING CHAPTER 30, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SEC-
4 TION 67-3015, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING THE IMMI-
5 GRATION STATUS AND NATIONALITY OF ARRESTED INDIVIDUALS; AND DECLARING
6 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. That Chapter 30, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
9 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
10 ignated as Section 67-3015, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
11 67-3015. IMMIGRATION STATUS AND NATIONALITY OF ARRESTED INDIVIDU-
12 ALS. (1) As used in this section:
13 (a) "Administration of criminal justice" means performance of any of
14 the following activities: detection, apprehension, detention, pre-
15 trial release, post-trial release, prosecution, adjudication, correc-
16 tional supervision, or rehabilitation of accused persons or criminal
17 offenders. The "administration of criminal justice" shall include
18 criminal identification activities and the collection, storage, and
19 dissemination of criminal history record information.
20 (b) "Arrest" means taking a person into custody in the manner autho-
21 rized by law by a peace officer or private citizen.
22 (c) "Immigration status" means a person's legal right to be in the
23 United States or lack thereof.
24 (d) "Law enforcement agency" means any law enforcement agency of the
25 state or any political subdivision of the state, including the Idaho
26 state police and any municipal police department or county sheriff's
27 department.
28 (2) In the performance of the administration of criminal justice, all
29 law enforcement agencies shall verify and record the immigration status and
30 nationality of any arrested individual.
31 (3) No later than October 1, 2025, each local, county, and state law en-
32 forcement agency shall record and publish a biannual report containing: the
33 immigration status and nationality of criminal offenders; crime statistics
34 related to foreign nationals arrested in Idaho, including the number of il-
35 legal aliens investigated, apprehended, detained, and transferred to fed-
36 eral officials; the crimes for which such persons were arrested, if any; and
37 any reason for noncompliance with the provisions of this section, if appli-
38 cable.
39 (4) Local, county, and state law enforcement agencies that fail to
40 comply with the provisions of this section may be subject to withholding of
41 state funding for their agency.
2
1 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
2 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
3 July 1, 2025.
