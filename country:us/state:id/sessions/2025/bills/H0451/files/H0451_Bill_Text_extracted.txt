Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 451
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT
3 PROGRAM'S DIVISION OF EDUCATIONAL SERVICES FOR THE DEAF AND THE BLIND;
4 APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATIONAL
5 SUPPORT PROGRAM'S DIVISION OF EDUCATIONAL SERVICES FOR THE DEAF AND
6 THE BLIND FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; APPROPRIATING GENERAL FUND MONEYS FOR
7 TRANSFER TO THE PUBLIC SCHOOL INCOME FUND; CLARIFYING THE ORIGINAL FUND
8 SOURCE FOR EXPENDITURES IN THE PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT PRO-
9 GRAM'S DIVISION OF EDUCATIONAL SERVICES FOR THE DEAF AND THE BLIND FOR
10 FISCAL YEAR 2026 FROM THE GENERAL FUND; CLARIFYING THE ORIGINAL FUND
11 SOURCE FOR EXPENDITURES IN THE PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT PRO-
12 GRAM'S DIVISION OF EDUCATIONAL SERVICES FOR THE DEAF AND THE BLIND FOR
13 FISCAL YEAR 2026 FROM THE SCHOOL FOR THE DEAF AND THE BLIND ENDOWMENT
14 FUND; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
15 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
16 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
17 there is hereby appropriated to the Public Schools Educational Support
18 Program's Division of Educational Services for the Deaf and the Blind the
19 following amounts to be expended from the listed funds for the period July 1,
20 2025, through June 30, 2026:
21 FROM:
22 Public School Income Fund $492,000
23 School for the Deaf and the Blind Endowment Fund 12,900
24 TOTAL $504,900
25 SECTION 2. There is hereby appropriated and the Office of the State Con-
26 troller shall transfer $492,000 from the General Fund to the Public School
27 Income Fund on July 1, 2025, or as soon thereafter as practicable for the pe-
28 riod July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
29 SECTION 3. Of the additional amounts appropriated in Section 1 of this
30 act for the Public Schools Educational Support Program's Division of Educa-
31 tional Services for the Deaf and the Blind $492,000 shall be considered ex-
32 pended from the General Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
33 2026.
34 SECTION 4. Of the additional amounts appropriated in Section 1 of this
35 act for the Public Schools Educational Support Program's Division of Educa-
36 tional Services for the Deaf and the Blind $12,900 shall be considered ex-
37 pended from the School for the Deaf and the Blind Endowment Fund for the pe-
38 riod July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
2
1 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
2 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
3 July 1, 2025.
