Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 365
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE ATTORNEY GENERAL; PROVIDING LEGISLATIVE INTENT; AMEND-
3 ING CHAPTER 14, TITLE 67, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SECTION
4 67-1417, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING CONCURRENT AU-
5 THORITY TO INVESTIGATE AND PROSECUTE ELECTION VIOLATIONS; AND DECLAR-
6 ING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the Legislature to
9 enhance the security and integrity of Idaho's elections by establishing
10 concurrent authority for the Attorney General to investigate and prosecute
11 election-related offenses within the State of Idaho. The Legislature seeks
12 to strengthen enforcement mechanisms, deter unlawful activities, and uphold
13 the rule of law in the electoral process.
14 SECTION 2. That Chapter 14, Title 67, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
15 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
16 ignated as Section 67-1417, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
17 67-1417. CONCURRENT AUTHORITY TO INVESTIGATE AND PROSECUTE ELECTION
18 VIOLATIONS. The attorney general may investigate and prosecute all criminal
19 and civil violations of chapter 23, title 18, Idaho Code, and title 34, Idaho
20 Code.
21 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
23 July 1, 2025.
