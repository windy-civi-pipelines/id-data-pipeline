LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
Moved by Ehlers
Seconded by Healey(15)
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE AMENDMENT TO H.B. NO. 359
1 AMENDMENT TO SECTION 2
2 On page 2 of the printed bill, delete lines 3 through 49.
3 On page 3, delete lines 1 through 6, and insert:
4 "(2) Every candidate in any partisan election shall disclose the polit-
5 ical party affiliation of such candidate, if any, in all election communica-
6 tions made by such candidate, except any election communication included on
7 stickers or any item designed to be worn by a person. The candidate's party
8 affiliation disclosure shall be prominently displayed within each election
9 communication. For the purposes of this section, "party affiliation" means
10 the written name of the political party such candidate is registered with or
11 a visual depiction of a commonly recognizable symbol that represents such
12 political party.".
