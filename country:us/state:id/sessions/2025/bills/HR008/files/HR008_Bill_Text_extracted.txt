Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 8
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING THAT ALL
3 TEMPORARY AND PENDING RULES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND WELFARE
4 AND THE DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL LICENSES HAVE BEEN
5 REVIEWED AND APPROVED BY THE HOUSE HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE, WITH
6 EXCEPTIONS.
7 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
9 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
10 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
11 letin; and
12 WHEREAS, the House Health and Welfare Committee reviewed temporary and
13 pending rules adopted by the Department of Health and Welfare and the Divi-
14 sion of Occupational and Professional Licenses.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
16 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
17 Legislature, that all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Department
18 of Health and Welfare and the Division of Occupational and Professional Li-
19 censes, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted through
20 the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for
21 review during the 2025 legislative session have been reviewed and approved
22 by the House Health and Welfare Committee, with the following exceptions
23 that were not approved:
24 (1) IDAPA 24.33.02, Division of Occupational and Professional Li-
25 censes, Rules for the Licensure of Physician Assistants, Docket No.
26 24-3302-2401, Section 100., Subsection 01., only; and
27 (2) IDAPA 24.36.01, Division of Occupational and Professional Li-
28 censes, Rules of the Idaho State Board of Pharmacy, Docket No.
29 24-3601-2402, Section 200., Subsection 14.e., only.
