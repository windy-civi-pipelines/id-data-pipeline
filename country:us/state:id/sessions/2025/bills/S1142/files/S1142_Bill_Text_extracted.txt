LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1142
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO EDUCATION; REPEALING SECTION 33-1029, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO
3 STATUTORY CONSTRUCTION AND NONPUBLIC SCHOOL STUDENTS; REPEALING SEC-
4 TION 33-1030, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO DEFINITIONS; REPEALING SECTION
5 33-1031, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE EMPOWERING PARENTS GRANT PROGRAM;
6 REPEALING SECTION 33-1032, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE PARENT ADVISORY
7 PANEL; REPEALING SECTION 33-1033, IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO A CERTAIN
8 REPORT AND EVALUATION; AMENDING SECTION 33-1034, IDAHO CODE, TO PRO-
9 VIDE FOR THE TRANSFER OF CERTAIN FUNDS; REPEALING SECTION 33-1034,
10 IDAHO CODE, RELATING TO THE EMPOWERING PARENTS GRANT PROGRAM FUND; AND
11 DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
12 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
13 SECTION 1. That Section 33-1029, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
14 repealed.
15 SECTION 2. That Section 33-1030, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
16 repealed.
17 SECTION 3. That Section 33-1031, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
18 repealed.
19 SECTION 4. That Section 33-1032, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
20 repealed.
21 SECTION 5. That Section 33-1033, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
22 repealed.
23 SECTION 6. That Section 33-1034, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
24 amended to read as follows:
25 33-1034. EMPOWERING PARENTS GRANT PROGRAM FUND. (1) There is hereby
26 established in the state treasury the empowering parents grant program fund,
27 to be administered by the board. Moneys in the fund shall:
28 (1) (a) Consist of the following:
29 (a) (i) Legislative appropriations;
30 (b) (ii) Donations and contributions made to the fund; and
31 (c) (iii) Interest earned on idle moneys in the fund;
32 (2) (b) Be continuously appropriated for the purpose described in sub-
33 section (3) of this section; and
34 (3) (c) Be used to pay grants awarded under the empowering parents grant
35 program.
36 (2) On June 20, 2025, or as soon thereafter as practicable , and no later
37 than June 29, 2025, the state controller shall transfer the unobligated cash
2
1 balance remaining in the empowering parents grant program fund into the gen-
2 eral fund.
3 SECTION 7. That Section 33-1034, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
4 repealed.
5 SECTION 8. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
6 declared to exist, Section 6 of this act shall be in full force and effect on
7 June 20, 2025, and Sections 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 7 of this act shall be in full
8 force and effect on and after June 30, 2025.
