Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 102
BY LOCAL GOVERNMENT AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 A SENATE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE SENATE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE TAX COMMISSION HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND APPROVED
4 BY THE SENATE LOCAL GOVERNMENT AND TAXATION COMMITTEE.
5 Be It Resolved by the Senate of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
7 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
8 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
9 letin; and
10 WHEREAS, the Senate Local Government and Taxation Committee reviewed
11 temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Tax Commission.
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the Senate, assembled
13 in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, that all
14 temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Tax Commission, pur-
15 suant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted through the Office
16 of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for review during
17 the 2025 legislative session have been reviewed and approved by the Senate
18 Local Government and Taxation Committee.
