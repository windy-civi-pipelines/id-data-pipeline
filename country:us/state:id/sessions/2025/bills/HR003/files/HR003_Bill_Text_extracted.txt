Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 3
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING FOR THE
3 AMENDMENT OF RULE 40 OF THE RULES OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES.
4 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
5 WHEREAS, the House of Representatives deems it necessary and desirable
6 that Rule 40 of the Rules of the House of Representatives be amended.
7 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
8 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
9 Legislature, that Rule 40 of the Rules of the House of Representatives shall
10 be amended to read as follows:
11 RULE 40
12 Session Decorum. -- (1) Smoking, vaping, and the use of any other
13 tobacco products and the consumption of food and beverage alcoholic bever-
14 ages will not be allowed in the Representative Chamber or gallery while the
15 House is in Session.
16 (2) Persons in the Chamber shall wear proper attire to maintain decorum
17 of the House.
