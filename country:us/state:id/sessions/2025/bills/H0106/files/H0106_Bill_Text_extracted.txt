Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 106
BY AGRICULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO COUNTY FAIR BOARDS; REPEALING SECTION 22-202A, IDAHO CODE, RE-
3 LATING TO THE DESIGNATION OF COUNTY FAIR BOARDS AS AN ADVISORY BODY;
4 AMENDING CHAPTER 2, TITLE 22, IDAHO CODE, BY THE ADDITION OF A NEW SEC-
5 TION 22-202A, IDAHO CODE, TO ESTABLISH PROVISIONS REGARDING ADVISORY
6 FAIR BOARDS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
7 DATE.
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
9 SECTION 1. That Section 22-202A, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
10 repealed.
11 SECTION 2. That Chapter 2, Title 22, Idaho Code, be, and the same is
12 hereby amended by the addition thereto of a NEW SECTION, to be known and des-
13 ignated as Section 22-202A, Idaho Code, and to read as follows:
14 22-202A. ADVISORY FAIR BOARDS. (1) Any fair board that serves as an ad-
15 visory body to the board of county commissioners may remain an advisory body
16 to the board of county commissioners or may petition to be a separate board
17 that operates pursuant to the provisions of this chapter.
18 (2) The petition shall contain the signatures of a majority of the advi-
19 sory fair board members and shall be submitted in writing to the clerk of the
20 board of county commissioners.
21 (3) Upon receipt of the petition, the board of county commissioners
22 shall immediately cause the clerk to publish a notice in a newspaper of gen-
23 eral circulation printed within the county for at least two (2) weeks, to the
24 effect that a petition to remove the fair board from its advisory capacity
25 and to allow the fair board to conduct a county fair in accordance with the
26 provisions of this chapter was received and that a hearing on the petition
27 will be held by the board of county commissioners on a date named in such
28 notice not less than three (3) and not more than six (6) weeks from the date of
29 the first publication of such notice.
30 (4) The fair board shall be removed from its advisory capacity and be
31 subject to the provisions of this chapter upon a majority vote of the board
32 of county commissioners and a three-quarters (3/4) majority vote of the fair
33 board members.
34 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
35 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
36 July 1, 2025.
