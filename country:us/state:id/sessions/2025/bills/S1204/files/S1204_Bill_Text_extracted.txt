Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1204
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR; AP-
3 PROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR
4 FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFEC-
5 TIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Executive Office of the Governor the
9 following amounts for the Administration Program to be expended according to
10 the designated expense classes from the General Fund for the period July 1,
11 2025, through June 30, 2026:
12 FOR:
13 Capital Outlay $45,000
14 Trustee and Benefit Payments 250,000
15 TOTAL $295,000
16 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
18 July 1, 2025.
