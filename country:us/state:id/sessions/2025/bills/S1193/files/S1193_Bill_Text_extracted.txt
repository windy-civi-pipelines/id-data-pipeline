LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1193
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE OFFICE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
3 SERVICES; APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE OFFICE OF INFORMA-
4 TION TECHNOLOGY SERVICES FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; INCREASING THE NUMBER
5 OF AUTHORIZED FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT POSITIONS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026;
6 PROVIDING FOR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY COST EFFICIENCIES REPORTING; PRO-
7 VIDING FOR CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS; APPROPRIATING
8 ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE OFFICE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SERVICES FOR
9 FISCAL YEAR 2025; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING EFFECTIVE
10 DATES.
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
12 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
13 there is hereby appropriated to the Office of Information Technology Ser-
14 vices the following amounts to be expended for the designated expense
15 classes from the listed funds for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
16 2026:
17 FOR FOR FOR
18 PERSONNEL OPERATING CAPITAL
19 COSTS EXPENDITURES OUTLAY TOTAL
20 FROM:
21 General
22 Fund $22,300 $3,092,300 $5,274,000 $8,388,600
23 Administration and Accounting Services
24 Fund 2,481,300 264,100 0 2,745,400
25 TOTAL $2,503,600 $3,356,400 $5,274,000 $11,134,000
26 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
27 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
28 to the Office of Information Technology Services is hereby increased by
29 twenty-two (22.00) for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
30 SECTION 3. INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY COST EFFICIENCIES REPORTING. The
31 Office of Information Technology Services and the Division of Financial
32 Management shall submit a report with recommendations for information tech-
33 nology equipment and services cost efficiencies. As part of this report,
34 the agencies shall include a survey of all executive branch state agency
35 information technology related expenditures and recommendations to support
36 the more efficient allocation of information technology related appropri-
37 ations. The Office of Information Technology Services and the Division of
38 Financial Management shall provide this report to the Joint Finance-Ap-
2
1 propriations Committee and the Budget and Policy Analysis Division of the
2 Legislative Services Office no later than December 1, 2025.
3 SECTION 4. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
4 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
5 strictions specified in this act.
6 SECTION 5. In addition to the appropriation made in Section 1, Chapter
7 55, Laws of 2024, and any other appropriation provided for by law, there
8 is hereby appropriated to the Office of Information Technology Services
9 $81,700 for operating expenditures from the General Fund for the period of
10 July 1, 2024, through June 30, 2025.
11 SECTION 6. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, Section 5 of this act shall be in full force and effect on
13 and after passage and approval, and Sections 1 through 4 of this act shall be
14 in full force and effect on and after July 1, 2025.
