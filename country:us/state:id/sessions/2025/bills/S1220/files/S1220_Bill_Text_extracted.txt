LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1220
BY FINANCE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO FUNDING OF THE OFFICE OF ENERGY AND MINERAL RESOURCES; APPROPRI-
3 ATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE OFFICE OF ENERGY AND MINERAL RESOURCES
4 FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; REDUCING THE APPROPRIATION TO THE OFFICE OF ENERGY
5 AND MINERAL RESOURCES FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY
6 AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
8 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
9 there is hereby appropriated to the Office of Energy and Mineral Resources
10 the following amounts from the listed funds for personnel costs for the pe-
11 riod July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026:
12 FROM:
13 Indirect Cost Recovery Fund $37,000
14 Miscellaneous Revenue Fund 11,300
15 TOTAL $48,300
16 SECTION 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary,
17 the appropriation made to the Office of Energy and Mineral Resources is
18 hereby reduced by the following amounts from the listed funds for personnel
19 costs for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026:
20 FROM:
21 Renewable Energy Resources Fund $30,400
22 Petroleum Price Violation Fund 17,900
23 TOTAL $48,300
24 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
25 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
26 July 1, 2025.
