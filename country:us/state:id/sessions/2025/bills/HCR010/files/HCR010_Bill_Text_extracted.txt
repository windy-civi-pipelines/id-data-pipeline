LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 10
BY JUDICIARY, RULES AND ADMINISTRATION COMMITTEE
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE LEGISLATURE AND MAKING THREE SEPARATE APPLICATIONS
3 TO THE UNITED STATES CONGRESS TO CALL A CONVENTION OF THE STATES UNDER
4 ARTICLE V OF THE CONSTITUTION OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE PURPOSE OF
5 PROPOSING AMENDMENTS TO THE CONSTITUTION OF THE UNITED STATES RELATED
6 TO BALANCING THE FEDERAL BUDGET, IMPOSING FISCAL RESTRAINTS ON THE
7 FEDERAL GOVERNMENT, LIMITING THE POWER AND JURISDICTION OF THE FEDERAL
8 GOVERNMENT, AND LIMITING THE TERMS OF OFFICE FOR ITS OFFICIALS AND FOR
9 MEMBERS OF CONGRESS.
10 Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
11 WHEREAS, the framers of the Constitution of the United States, through
12 the enactment of Article V, empowered state legislators to be guardians of
13 liberty against future abuses of power by the federal government; and
14 WHEREAS, the federal government has created a crushing national debt
15 through improper and imprudent spending; and
16 WHEREAS, the federal government has invaded the legitimate roles of the
17 states through the manipulative process of federal mandates, most of which
18 are unfunded to a great extent; and
19 WHEREAS, the federal government has ceased to live under a proper inter-
20 pretation of the Constitution of the United States; and
21 WHEREAS, it is the solemn duty of the states to protect the liberty of
22 our people, particularly for the generations to come, by proposing amend-
23 ments to the Constitution of the United States through a convention of the
24 states under Article V of the Constitution of the United States to place
25 clear restraints on these and related abuses of power; and
26 WHEREAS, the citizens of the State of Idaho believe that it is in the
27 best interest of the people of the United States to amend the Constitution
28 of the United States in order to adopt a balanced budget amendment and to ad-
29 dress the areas of overreach of the federal government; and
30 WHEREAS, as early as 1979, the Forty-fifth Idaho Legislature enacted
31 House Concurrent Resolution No. 7, calling for an Article V Convention for
32 the purpose of preparing and submitting to the states an amendment "to re-
33 quire, with certain exceptions, that the total of all federal appropriations
34 may not exceed the total of all estimated federal revenues in any fiscal
35 year"; and
36 WHEREAS, Article V of the Constitution of the United States mandates
37 that upon the "Application of the Legislatures of two thirds of the several
38 States" Congress shall "call a Convention for proposing Amendments."
39 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the First Regular Ses-
40 sion of the Sixty-eighth Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and
41 the Senate concurring therein, that the Legislature of the State of Idaho
42 hereby makes three separate applications to Congress, under the provisions
43 of Article V of the Constitution of the United States. The first such appli-
2
1 cation is set forth in Sections 1 through 3 of this resolution. The second
2 such application is set forth in Sections 4 through 6 of this resolution. The
3 third such application is set forth in Sections 7 through 10 of this resolu-
4 tion.
5 SECTION 1. The members of the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth
6 Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and Senate concurring
7 therein, as the Legislature of the State of Idaho, hereby apply to Congress,
8 under the provisions of Article V of the Constitution of the United States,
9 for the calling of a convention of the states limited to proposing an amend-
10 ment to the Constitution of the United States requiring that in the absence
11 of a national emergency that the total of all Federal outlays made by the Con-
12 gress for any fiscal year may not exceed the total of all estimated Federal
13 revenues for that fiscal year, together with any related and appropriate
14 fiscal restraints.
15 SECTION 2. The Secretary of State is hereby directed to transmit copies
16 of this application to the President and Secretary of the United States Sen-
17 ate, the Speaker and Clerk of the United States House of Representatives, and
18 the members of the United States Senate and the United States House of Rep-
19 resentatives from this state. The Secretary of State is further directed to
20 transmit copies of this resolution to the presiding officers of each of the
21 legislative houses in the several states, requesting their cooperation in
22 this endeavor.
23 SECTION 3. This application constitutes a continuing application in
24 accordance with Article V of the Constitution of the United States until the
25 legislatures of at least two-thirds of the several states have made appli-
26 cations on the same subject, or until December 31, 2031, whichever occurs
27 earlier. This application supersedes all previous applications by this
28 Legislature on the same subject.
29 SECTION 4. The members of the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth
30 Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and Senate concurring
31 therein, as the Legislature of the State of Idaho, hereby apply to Congress,
32 under the provisions of Article V of the Constitution of the United States,
33 for the calling of a convention of the states limited to proposing amend-
34 ments to the Constitution of the United States that impose fiscal restraints
35 on the federal government, limit the power and jurisdiction of the federal
36 government and limit the terms of office for its officials and for members of
37 Congress.
38 SECTION 5. The Secretary of State is hereby directed to transmit copies
39 of this application to the President and Secretary of the United States Sen-
40 ate, the Speaker and Clerk of the United States House of Representatives, and
41 the members of the United States Senate and the United States House of Rep-
42 resentatives from this state. The Secretary of State is further directed to
43 transmit copies to the presiding officers of each of the legislative houses
44 in the several states, requesting their cooperation.
45 SECTION 6. This application constitutes a continuing application in
46 accordance with Article V of the Constitution of the United States until the
47 legislatures of at least two-thirds of the several states have made appli-
48 cations on the same subject, or until December 31, 2031, whichever occurs
49 earlier. This application supersedes all previous applications by this
50 Legislature on the same subject.
3
1 SECTION 7. The members of the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth
2 Idaho Legislature, the House of Representatives and Senate concurring
3 therein, as the Legislature of the State of Idaho, hereby apply to Congress,
4 under the provisions of Article V of the Constitution of the United States,
5 for the calling of a convention of the states limited to proposing an amend-
6 ment to the Constitution of the United States to set a limit on the number of
7 terms that a person may be elected as a Member of the United States House of
8 Representatives and to set a limit on the number of terms that a person may be
9 elected as a Member of the United States Senate.
10 SECTION 8. The Secretary of State is hereby directed to transmit copies
11 of this application to the President and Secretary of the United States Sen-
12 ate, the Speaker and Clerk of the United States House of Representatives and
13 members of the United States Senate and House of Representatives from this
14 state. The Secretary of State is further directed to transmit copies to the
15 presiding officers of each of the legislative houses in the several states,
16 requesting their cooperation.
17 SECTION 9. This application shall be considered as covering the same
18 subject matter as the applications from other states to Congress to call a
19 convention to set a limit on the number of terms that a person may be elected
20 to the House of Representatives of the Congress of the United States and the
21 Senate of the United States, and this application shall be aggregated with
22 the same for the purpose of attaining the two-thirds of states necessary to
23 require Congress to call a limited convention on this subject but shall not
24 be aggregated with any other applications on any other subject.
25 SECTION 10. This application constitutes a continuing application in
26 accordance with Article V of the Constitution of the United States until the
27 legislatures of at least two-thirds of the several states have made applica-
28 tions on the same subject, or until December 31, 2031, whichever occurs ear-
29 lier. This application supersedes all previous applications by this Legis-
30 lature on the same subject.
