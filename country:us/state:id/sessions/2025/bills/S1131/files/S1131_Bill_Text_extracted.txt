1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DEPARTMENT OF INSURANCE; APPROPRIATING
3 ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF INSURANCE FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026;
4 AUTHORIZING ADDITIONAL FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT POSITIONS; AND DECLARING
5 AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
7 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
8 there is hereby appropriated to the Department of Insurance the following
9 amounts to be expended according to the designated programs and expense
10 classes from the listed funds for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
11 2026:
12 FOR FOR FOR
13 PERSONNEL OPERATING CAPITAL
14 COSTS EXPENDITURES OUTLAY TOTAL
15 I. INSURANCE REGULATION:
16 FROM:
17 Insurance Administrative
18 Fund $317,100 $6,000 $323,100
19 II. STATE FIRE MARSHAL:
20 FROM:
21 Arson, Fire and Fraud Prevention
22 Fund $48,100 $162,200 $210,300
23 GRAND TOTAL $365,200 $6,000 $162,200 $533,400
24 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
25 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
26 to the Department of Insurance is hereby increased by two (2.00) for the pe-
27 riod July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
28 SECTION 3. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
29 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
30 July 1, 2025.
