Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 212
BY LOCAL GOVERNMENT COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PUBLIC LIBRARY DISTRICTS; AMENDING SECTION 33-2704, IDAHO CODE,
3 TO REVISE A PROVISION REGARDING PETITIONING TO PROPOSE A LIBRARY DIS-
4 TRICT; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 33-2704, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 33-2704. PETITION -- VERIFICATION -- NOTICE AND HEARING. (1) A peti-
9 tion or petitions, signed by fifty (50) or more not less than twenty percent
10 (20%) of the qualified electors residing in who reside within the boundaries
11 of the proposed library district, giving the name of the proposed district,
12 describing the boundaries thereof including a map prepared in a draftsman-
13 like manner, and praying for the establishment of the territory therein de-
14 scribed as a public library district, shall be filed with the clerk or clerks
15 of the boards of county commissioners of the counties in which the proposed
16 district is situated.
17 The petition or petitions shall be verified by at least one (1) quali-
18 fied elector, which verification shall state that the affiant knows that all
19 of the parties whose names are signed to the petition are qualified electors
20 of the proposed district, and that their signatures to the petition were made
21 in his presence. The verification may be made before any notary public.
22 (2) When the petition or petitions are presented to the board of county
23 commissioners and filed in the office of the clerk of the board, the board
24 shall set the time for a hearing, which time shall be not less than three (3)
25 nor more than six (6) weeks from the date of the presentation and filing of
26 the petition. Notice of the time of hearing shall be published by the board
27 at least once a week for two (2) weeks prior to the time set for the hearing,
28 in a newspaper of general circulation within the county in which the proposed
29 district is situated.
30 (3) The notice shall state that a library district is proposed to be
31 established, giving the proposed boundaries and name thereof, and that any
32 resident elector within the proposed boundaries of the proposed district may
33 appear and be heard in regard to:
34 (a) The form of the petition;
35 (b) The genuineness of the signatures;
36 (c) The legality of the proceedings; and
37 (d) Any other matters in regard to the creation of the library district.
38 (4) Concurrently with the notice of hearing, the board of county com-
39 missioners shall notify, in writing, the governing body of any tax supported
40 library within the boundaries of the proposed library district. If any gov-
41 erning body decides that it is not in the best interest of library services to
42 be included within the proposed library district, they shall present a res-
2
1 olution stating this to the county commissioners, not less than one (1) week
2 prior to the date of hearing.
3 (5) No later than ten (10) days after the hearing, the board of county
4 commissioners shall make an order thereon with or without modification,
5 based upon the public hearing and their determination of whether the pro-
6 posed library district would be in keeping with the declared public policy
7 of the state of Idaho in regard to library districts as more particularly set
8 forth in section 33-2701, Idaho Code, and, shall accordingly fix the bound-
9 aries and certify the name of the proposed district in the order granting the
10 petition. The boundaries so fixed shall be the boundaries of the district
11 after its establishment is completed as provided in this chapter.
12 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
14 July 1, 2025.
