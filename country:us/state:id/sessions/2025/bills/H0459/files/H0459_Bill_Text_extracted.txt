IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 459
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR; APPROPRIATING
3 ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; RE-
4 DUCING THE APPROPRIATION TO THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR FOR FISCAL YEAR
5 2026; REDUCING THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT POSI-
6 TIONS; APPROPRIATING AND TRANSFERRING MONEYS FROM THE EMPLOYMENT
7 SECURITY ADMINISTRATION AND REIMBURSEMENT FUND TO THE EMPLOYMENT SE-
8 CURITY FUND; PROVIDING FOR A FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT FOR THE FEDERAL
9 GRANT FUND; PROVIDING FOR A FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT FOR THE EMPLOYMENT
10 SECURITY SPECIAL ADMINISTRATION FUND; PROVIDING FOR A FUND BALANCE AD-
11 JUSTMENT FOR THE EMPLOYMENT SECURITY FUND; PROVIDING FOR A FUND BALANCE
12 ADJUSTMENT FOR THE EMPLOYMENT SECURITY RESERVE FUND; PROVIDING FOR A
13 REPORT ON FULL-TIME POSITIONS AND VACANCIES; PROVIDING FOR A REPORT ON
14 ILLEGAL IMMIGRATION AND E-VERIFY; PROVIDING FOR A REPORT ON DISABILITY
15 DETERMINATION SERVICES; PROVIDING FOR CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RE-
16 STRICTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.

18 provided by law to the contrary, the full-time equivalent position autho-
19 rization provided to the Department of Labor is hereby reduced by fifteen and
20 fifty-eight hundredths (15.58) for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
21 2026.
22 SECTION 4. CASH TRANSFER. There is hereby appropriated and the Office
23 of the State Controller shall transfer up to $4,868,600 from the Employment
24 Security Administration and Reimbursement Fund to the Employment Security
25 Fund on July 1, 2025, or as soon thereafter as practicable for the period July
26 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
27 SECTION 5. FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT. Pursuant to Section 67-3604, Idaho
28 Code, authority is hereby granted to the Idaho Department of Labor and the
29 Office of the State Controller to initiate system actions that will cor-
30 rect the fund balance in the Federal Grant Fund by an amount not to exceed
31 $12,477,700.
32 SECTION 6. FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT. Pursuant to Section 67-3604, Idaho
33 Code, authority is hereby granted to the Idaho Department of Labor and the
34 Office of the State Controller to initiate system actions that will correct
35 the fund balance in the Employment Security Special Administration Fund by
36 an amount not to exceed $2,509,900.
37 SECTION 7. FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT. Pursuant to Section 67-3604, Idaho
38 Code, authority is hereby granted to the Idaho Department of Labor and the
39 Office of the State Controller to record an accounting entry reducing the
3
1 balance in the Employment Security Fund by an amount not to exceed $4,047,900
2 and to make the necessary accounting adjustments to any impacted accounts
3 related thereto.
4 SECTION 8. FUND BALANCE ADJUSTMENT. Pursuant to Section 67-3604, Idaho
5 Code, authority is hereby granted to the Idaho Department of Labor and the
6 Office of the State Controller to record an accounting entry increasing the
7 balance in the Investment Balance in the Employment Security Reserve Fund by
8 an amount not to exceed $15,511,300 and to make the necessary accounting ad-
9 justments to any impacted accounts related thereto.
10 SECTION 9. REPORT ON FULL-TIME POSITIONS AND VACANCIES. The Department
11 of Labor shall provide a report to the Budget and Policy Analysis Division
12 of the Legislative Services Office on current authorized positions, filled
13 positions, active positions, length of vacancy for unfilled positions, and
14 salary savings for fiscal year 2025. This report shall be submitted no later
15 than September 30, 2025.
16 SECTION 10. REPORT ON ILLEGAL IMMIGRATION AND E-VERIFY. The Department
17 of Labor shall provide a report including an analysis of the impact of il-
18 legal immigration on the state's labor market and the potential costs and
19 benefits of using E-Verify. The report shall be submitted to the Joint Fi-
20 nance-Appropriations Committee and the Budget and Policy Analysis Division
21 of the Legislative Services Office no later than December 15, 2025.
22 SECTION 11. REPORT ON DISABILITY DETERMINATION SERVICES. The Depart-
23 ment of Labor shall provide a report including a review of the costs and ben-
24 efits of administering the Disability Determination Services at the state
25 level vs. the federal level and the law changes that are necessary to remove
26 state level determination. The report shall be submitted to the Joint Fi-
27 nance-Appropriations Committee and the Budget and Policy Analysis Division
28 of the Legislative Services Office no later than December 15, 2025.
29 SECTION 12. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
30 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
31 strictions specified in this act.
32 SECTION 13. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
33 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
34 July 1, 2025.
