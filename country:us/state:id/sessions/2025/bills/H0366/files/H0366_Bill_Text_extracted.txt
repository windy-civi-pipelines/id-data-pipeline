Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 366
BY HEALTH AND WELFARE COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO PUBLIC HEALTH DISTRICTS; AMENDING SECTION 39-411, IDAHO CODE, TO
3 REVISE PROVISIONS REGARDING THE COMPOSITION OF HEALTH DISTRICT BOARDS;
4 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 39-411, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 39-411. COMPOSITION OF DISTRICT BOARD -- QUALIFICATIONS OF MEMBERS
9 -- APPOINTMENT AND REMOVAL -- TERMS -- SELECTION OF OFFICERS -- BOARD OF
10 TRUSTEES OF DISTRICT BOARDS OF HEALTH. (1) For those districts comprised of:
11 (a) Fewer than eight (8) counties, the district board of health shall
12 consist of seven (7) members to be appointed by each of the boards of
13 county commissioners within each district acting jointly, and, and each
14 board of county commissioners may appoint a county commissioner of such
15 county as a board member. Any remaining board seats shall be filled in
16 order as follows:
17 (i) The boards of county commissioners within each district act-
18 ing jointly shall appoint a physician licensed by the Idaho state
19 board of medicine, if available;
20 (ii) The board of county commissioners for the county that pro-
21 vides the greatest amount of funding for the district pursuant to
22 section 39-424, Idaho Code, shall appoint a member to an available
23 board seat; and
24 (iii) All remaining board seats shall be filled by the boards of
25 county commissioners within each district acting jointly.
26 (b) Eight (8) counties, the district board of health shall consist of
27 not fewer than eight (8) members nor more than nine (9) members, and each
28 board of county commissioners may appoint a county commissioner of such
29 county as a board member. The boards of county commissioners within
30 each district acting jointly may also appoint a physician licensed by
31 the Idaho state board of medicine, if available.
32 (2)(a) Each member of the district board of health shall be a citizen of
33 the United States, a resident of the state of Idaho and the public health
34 district for one (1) year immediately last past, and a qualified elec-
35 tor. One (1) member of the district board, if available to serve, shall
36 be a physician licensed by the Idaho state board of medicine. All mem-
37 bers shall be chosen with due regard to their knowledge and interest in
38 public health and in promoting the health of the citizens of the state
39 and the public health district. Representation shall be assured from
40 rural as well as urban population groups.
2
1 (b) Any member appointed pursuant to subsection (1)(a)(ii) of this sec-
2 tion shall be a currently elected county official from the county making
3 the appointment.
4 (c) Any member appointed pursuant to subsection (1)(a)(iii) of this
5 section shall be a currently elected county official from a county
6 within the district.
7 (3) All appointments The appointment of a physician to the district
8 board, and any appointments to the board pursuant to subsection (1)(a)(iii)
9 of this section shall be confirmed by a majority vote of all the county com-
10 missioners of all the counties located within the public health district.
11 Any member of the district board Board members appointed pursuant to sub-
12 section (1)(a)(iii) of this section may be removed by majority at least
13 two-thirds (2/3) vote of all the county commissioners of all the counties
14 located within the district.
15 (4) The members of the district board of health shall be appointed for
16 a term of five (5) two (2) years, subject to reappointment; and vacancies on
17 the board for an unexpired term shall be filled for the balance of the un-
18 expired term. Notwithstanding any provision of this section as to term of
19 appointment, if a board member is an appointee for a board of county com-
20 missioners, and if that board member is an elected county commissioner and
21 leaves office prior to the expiration of the term on the district board of
22 health, the board of county commissioners may declare the position vacant
23 and may appoint another currently elected county commissioner to fill the
24 unexpired portion of the term of that board member.
25 (5) The members of the district board, each year, shall select a chair-
26 man, a vice chairman and a trustee. The trustee shall represent the district
27 board as a member of the board of trustees of the Idaho district boards of
28 health.
29 (6) The board of trustees of the Idaho district boards of health shall
30 have authority to allocate appropriations from the legislature to the health
31 districts. Such authority is limited to the development and administration
32 of formulas for the allocation of legislative appropriations. Any formula
33 adopted by the board of trustees must be in use, without alteration, for at
34 least two (2) years; provided that during the two (2) year period, the for-
35 mula may be changed if an emergency occurs, the emergency is declared and
36 there is a unanimous vote of the board of trustees to make the emergency for-
37 mula change. All proceedings of the board of trustees shall be subject to the
38 provisions of chapter 2, title 74, Idaho Code.
39 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
40 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
41 July 1, 2025.
