Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE SENATE
SENATE BILL NO. 1121
BY STATE AFFAIRS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO COUNTIES; AMENDING SECTION 31-2808, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE PROVI-
3 SIONS REGARDING PROHIBITING THE FINAL DISPOSITION OF DEAD HUMAN BODIES;
4 AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE DATE.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 31-2808, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 31-2808. MAKING FINAL DISPOSITION OF DEAD HUMAN BODIES PROHIBITED. No
9 coroner or person acting as coroner who is a licensed funeral director or
10 a licensed mortician, owner, or proprietor, or employee of any establish-
11 ment engaged in making final disposition of dead human bodies, and no estab-
12 lishment with which such coroner or person acting as coroner is associated,
13 shall, except for ambulance services, perform any of the services of a fu-
14 neral director or mortician or furnish any materials connected with or in-
15 cidental to the final disposition of the body of any person whose death is
16 required by law to be investigated by such coroner or other person acting in
17 that capacity. Any person who violates this section shall be guilty of a mis-
18 demeanor. Provided, however, that the provisions of this section shall not
19 be applicable in counties wherein there is only one (1) licensed funeral es-
20 tablishment or in counties with a population of fewer than twenty thousand
21 (20,000) people according to the most recent federal census.
22 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
23 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
24 July 1, 2025.
