LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 372
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE STATE APPELLATE PUBLIC DEFENDER; AP-
3 PROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE STATE APPELLATE PUBLIC DEFENDER
4 FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; INCREASING THE NUMBER OF AUTHORIZED FULL-TIME
5 EQUIVALENT POSITIONS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; PROVIDING REAPPROPRIATION
6 AUTHORITY FOR CAPITAL CASES; PROVIDING REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR
7 NONCAPITAL APPEALS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFEC-
8 TIVE DATE.
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
10 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
11 there is hereby appropriated to the State Appellate Public Defender for the
12 Office of the State Appellate Public Defender program the following amounts
13 to be expended according to the designated expense classes from the General
14 Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026:
15 FOR:
16 Personnel Costs $129,500
17 Operating Expenditures 8,200
18 Capital Outlay 2,100
19 TOTAL $139,800
20 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
21 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
22 to the State Appellate Public Defender is hereby increased by one (1.00) for
23 the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
24 SECTION 3. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY. There is hereby reappropriated
25 to the State Appellate Public Defender any unexpended and unencumbered
26 balances appropriated or reappropriated to the State Appellate Public De-
27 fender from the General Fund for fiscal year 2025, in an amount not to exceed
28 $1,209,400 from the General Fund for costs directly related to the provision
29 of representation in capital cases, to be used for nonrecurring expendi-
30 tures for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026. The Office of the
31 State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation amount, by fund, expense
32 class, and program, with the Legislative Services Office prior to processing
33 the reappropriation authorized herein.
34 SECTION 4. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY. There is hereby reappropriated
35 to the State Appellate Public Defender any unexpended and unencumbered bal-
36 ances appropriated or reappropriated to the State Appellate Public Defender
37 from the General Fund for fiscal year 2025 from the General Fund for costs di-
38 rectly related to outside counsel for noncapital appeals, to be used for non-
2
1 recurring expenditures for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
2 The Office of the State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation amount,
3 by fund, expense class, and program, with the Legislative Services Office
4 prior to processing the reappropriation authorized herein.
5 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
6 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
7 July 1, 2025.
