Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 374
BY APPROPRIATIONS COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO THE APPROPRIATION TO THE AGRICULTURAL RESEARCH AND EXTENSION
3 SERVICE; APPROPRIATING ADDITIONAL MONEYS TO THE AGRICULTURAL RESEARCH
4 AND EXTENSION SERVICE FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; AUTHORIZING ADDITIONAL
5 FULL-TIME EQUIVALENT POSITIONS FOR FISCAL YEAR 2026; PROVIDING FOR
6 PARTNERSHIPS WITH INDUSTRY; PROVIDING FOR CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND
7 RESTRICTIONS; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING AN EFFECTIVE
8 DATE.
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
10 SECTION 1. In addition to any other appropriation provided by law,
11 there is hereby appropriated to the Agricultural Research and Extension
12 Service the following amounts to be expended according to the designated
13 expense classes from the General Fund for the period July 1, 2025, through
14 June 30, 2026:
15 FOR:
16 Personnel Costs $19,300
17 Operating Expenditures 230,800
18 TOTAL $250,100
19 SECTION 2. FTP AUTHORIZATION. In addition to any other authorization
20 provided by law, the full-time equivalent position authorization provided
21 to the Agricultural Research and Extension Service is hereby increased by
22 forty-three hundredths (0.43) for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30,
23 2026.
24 SECTION 3. STRENGTHENING PARTNERSHIPS WITH INDUSTRY. The Agricul-
25 tural Research and Extension Service shall provide the Legislature with a
26 long-term financial operations plan for the Center for Agriculture, Food,
27 and the Environment (CAFE), including a detailed accounting of anticipated
28 expenses and sources of revenue. The financial operations plan should fo-
29 cus on strengthening partnerships with Idaho's dairy, ranching, and other
30 agricultural industries, including partnerships that might replace General
31 Fund expenditures in future fiscal years. The Agricultural Research and
32 Extension Service shall submit the plan to the Joint Finance-Appropriations
33 Committee by December 31, 2025. The format and content of the financial op-
34 erations plan will be determined in consultation with the Budget and Policy
35 Analysis Division of the Legislative Services Office.
36 SECTION 4. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
37 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
38 strictions specified in this act.
2
1 SECTION 5. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
2 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after
3 July 1, 2025.
