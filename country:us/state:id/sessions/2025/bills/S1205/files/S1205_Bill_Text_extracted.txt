10 SECTION 3. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR THE CAPITAL FACILITIES PRO-
11 GRAM. There is hereby reappropriated to the Idaho Transportation Department
12 any unexpended and unencumbered balances appropriated to the Idaho Trans-
13 portation Department from the State Aeronautics (Dedicated) Fund and the
14 State Highway (Dedicated) Fund in the Capital Facilities Program for fiscal
15 year 2025, to be used for nonrecurring expenditures in the Capital Facili-
16 ties Program for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026. The Office
17 of the State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation amount, by fund,
18 expense class, and program, with the Legislative Services Office prior to
19 processing the reappropriation authorized herein.
20 SECTION 4. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR AIRPORT DEVELOPMENT GRANTS.
21 There is hereby reappropriated to the Idaho Transportation Department any
22 unexpended and unencumbered balances appropriated or reappropriated to the
23 Idaho Transportation Department from the State Aeronautics (Dedicated) Fund
24 as trustee and benefit payments for airport development grants for fiscal
25 year 2025, to be used for nonrecurring expenditures for airport development
26 grants for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026. The Office of the
27 State Controller shall confirm the reappropriation amount, by fund, expense
28 class, and program, with the Legislative Services Office prior to processing
29 the reappropriation authorized herein.
30 SECTION 5. REAPPROPRIATION AUTHORITY FOR THE CONTRACT CONSTRUCTION
31 AND RIGHT-OF-WAY ACQUISITION DIVISION. There is hereby reappropriated to
32 the Idaho Transportation Department any unexpended and unencumbered bal-
33 ances appropriated or reappropriated to the Idaho Transportation Department
34 from the State Highway Fund and the Transportation Expansion and Congestion
35 Mitigation Fund for the Contract Construction and Right-of-Way Acquisition
36 Division for fiscal year 2025, in a total amount not to exceed $300,000,000
37 from the State Highway Fund and/or the Transportation Expansion and Con-
38 gestion Mitigation Fund, to be used for nonrecurring expenditures for the
39 Contract Construction and Right-of-Way Acquisition Division for the period
40 July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026. The Office of the State Controller shall
41 confirm the reappropriation amount, by fund, expense class, and program,
42 with the Legislative Services Office prior to processing the reappropria-
43 tion authorized herein.

1 SECTION 6. CASH TRANSFER. There is hereby appropriated, and the Office
2 of the State Controller shall transfer $165,000,000 from the General Fund to
3 the Strategic Initiatives Program (Dedicated) Fund as soon as practicable
4 for the period July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
5 SECTION 7. CASH TRANSFER. There is hereby appropriated, and the Office
6 of the State Controller shall transfer $110,000,000 from the General Fund
7 to the Local Highway Distribution Fund as soon as practicable for the period
8 July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
9 SECTION 8. LOCAL HIGHWAY DISTRIBUTION FUND. The amount appropriated in
10 Section 7 of this act shall be distributed in full with the first distribu-
11 tion from the Highway Distribution Account.
12 SECTION 9. ALLOCATION OF FUNDS FOR SPECIFIC PROJECTS. Notwithstanding
13 the provisions of any law to the contrary, of the moneys distributed pursuant
14 to Section 63-3638(16), Idaho Code, and apportioned to local units of gov-
15 ernment in the same percentages provided for in Section 40-709(1) through
16 (3), Idaho Code, to any single countywide highway district formed pursuant
17 to Chapter 14, Title 40, Idaho Code, at least $8,000,000 shall be used to-
18 wards the construction of a road widening project and bridge reconstruction
19 for congestion mitigation on a residential mobility arterial that connects a
20 State Highway to a U.S. Highway. For purposes of this section, "residential
21 mobility arterial" means a roadway identified as a residential mobility ar-
22 terial pursuant to each district's master street map as of June 26, 2024.
23 SECTION 10. ALLOCATION OF FUNDS FOR SPECIFIC PROJECTS. Notwithstand-
24 ing the provisions of any law to the contrary, of the moneys transferred in
25 Section 7 of this act and apportioned to local units of government in the
26 same percentages provided for in Section 40-709, Idaho Code, to any single
27 countywide highway district formed pursuant to Chapter 14, Title 40, Idaho
28 Code, at least $19,200,000 shall be used towards the construction of a road
29 widening project and bridge reconstruction for congestion mitigation on a
30 residential mobility arterial that connects a State Highway to a U.S. High-
31 way. For purposes of this section, "residential mobility arterial" means a
32 roadway identified as a residential mobility arterial pursuant to each dis-
33 trict's master street map as of June 26, 2024.
34 SECTION 11. AIRPORT DEVELOPMENT GRANT ELIGIBILITY. Airport develop-
35 ment grants shall not be allocated to airports in violation of the Policy on
36 the Non-Aeronautical Use of Airport Hangars, 81 F.R. 38906. Compliance with
37 the federal regulation will be confirmed by the airport sponsor and reported
38 to the Idaho Transportation Department prior to distribution of funds.
39 SECTION 12. CONDITIONS, LIMITATIONS, AND RESTRICTIONS. Appropriations
40 made pursuant to this act are subject to the conditions, limitations, and re-
41 strictions specified in this act.
