Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 7
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDING THAT ALL
3 PENDING RULES OF THE IDAHO TRANSPORTATION DEPARTMENT HAVE BEEN REVIEWED
4 AND APPROVED BY THE HOUSE TRANSPORTATION COMMITTEE.
5 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
6 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
7 of the Legislature shall review any pending rule that is germane to its com-
8 mittee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bulletin; and
9 WHEREAS, the House Transportation Committee reviewed pending rules
10 adopted by the Idaho Transportation Department.
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
12 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
13 Legislature, that all pending rules adopted by the Idaho Transportation
14 Department, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted
15 through the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legisla-
16 ture for review during the 2025 legislative session have been reviewed and
17 approved by the House Transportation Committee.
