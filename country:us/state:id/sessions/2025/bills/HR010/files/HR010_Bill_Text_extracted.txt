LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 10
BY WAYS AND MEANS COMMITTEE
1 A HOUSE RESOLUTION
2 STATING FINDINGS OF THE HOUSE AND PROVIDING THAT ALL TEMPORARY AND PENDING
3 RULES OF THE IDAHO STATE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE, THE IDAHO SHEEP
4 AND GOAT HEALTH BOARD, THE DIVISION OF OCCUPATIONAL AND PROFESSIONAL
5 LICENSES, AND THE IDAHO OILSEED COMMISSION HAVE BEEN REVIEWED AND AP-
6 PROVED BY THE HOUSE AGRICULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE.
7 Be It Resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of Idaho:
8 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 67-5291, Idaho Code, standing committees
9 of the Legislature shall review any temporary or pending rule that is ger-
10 mane to its committee and has been published in the Idaho Administrative Bul-
11 letin; and
12 WHEREAS, the House Agricultural Affairs Committee reviewed temporary
13 and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State Department of Agriculture, the
14 Idaho Sheep and Goat Health Board, the Division of Occupational and Profes-
15 sional Licenses, and the Idaho Oilseed Commission.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the members of the House of Represen-
17 tatives, assembled in the First Regular Session of the Sixty-eighth Idaho
18 Legislature, that all temporary and pending rules adopted by the Idaho State
19 Department of Agriculture, the Idaho Sheep and Goat Health Board, the Divi-
20 sion of Occupational and Professional Licenses, and the Idaho Oilseed Com-
21 mission, pursuant to the Administrative Procedure Act and submitted through
22 the Office of the Administrative Rules Coordinator to the Legislature for
23 review during the 2025 legislative session have been reviewed and approved
24 by the House Agricultural Affairs Committee.
