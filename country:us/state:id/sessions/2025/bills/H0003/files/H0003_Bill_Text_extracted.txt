Section 1:
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF IDAHO
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
HOUSE BILL NO. 3
BY REVENUE AND TAXATION COMMITTEE
1 AN ACT
2 RELATING TO TAXATION; AMENDING SECTION 63-3004, IDAHO CODE, TO REVISE A DEF-
3 INITION; AND DECLARING AN EMERGENCY AND PROVIDING RETROACTIVE APPLICA-
4 TION.
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Idaho:
6 SECTION 1. That Section 63-3004, Idaho Code, be, and the same is hereby
7 amended to read as follows:
8 63-3004. INTERNAL REVENUE CODE. The term "Internal Revenue Code"
9 means the Internal Revenue Code as amended and in effect on the first day of
10 January 2024 2025, except that Internal Revenue Code section 85 is applied
11 as in effect on January 1, 2020.
12 SECTION 2. An emergency existing therefor, which emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, this act shall be in full force and effect on and after its
14 passage and approval, and retroactively to January 1, 2025.
